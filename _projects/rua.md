Rua is an AI-powered personal assistant designed to boost productivity and streamline daily tasks.

- **Chat Integration**: Handle messages via Telegram.
- **Google Calendar Integration**: Interact with Google Calendar to manage events.
- **Real-time Event Notifications**: Webhook-based push notifications for calendar events.
- **Daily Agenda Management**: Automatically shows your daily agenda once a day.
- **Daily report**: Automatically send you a markdown recap of your day.
- **Audio Transcription**: Convert audio files to text using whisper.
- **Photo Management**: (Work in progress)
